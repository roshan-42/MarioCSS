It's me, Mario

The Mario game series, created by Nintendo, is an iconic staple of the gaming world. Centered around the adventures of the mustachioed plumber Mario, players navigate through colorful worlds, overcoming obstacles, stomping on enemies, and rescuing Princess Peach from the clutches of the villainous Bowser. From the groundbreaking platformer Super Mario Bros. released in 1985 to modern titles like Super Mario Odyssey, which offers expansive 3D environments and innovative gameplay mechanics, the series has continually evolved while maintaining its beloved core elements. With its memorable characters, catchy music, and timeless gameplay, Mario games have captured the hearts of players of all ages for generations.
